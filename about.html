About me

Wher I work

My work area is Västergötland and Örebro County, starting from Töreboda. I shoe and work all types of horses from hobby/forest horses to high level competition horses in all disciplines

Education

Academy of Crafts Stockholm 2 years

The vocational school Hantverksakademin has developed a modern craft education with clear guidelines and a solid apprenticeship.

Biological Vocational School Skara 1 year

BYS farrier school in Skara was the first in Sweden to be certified by EFFA, the European Federation of Farrier Association. They continue to be at the forefront of teaching and quality as they have been since the school was started in the 18th century.

Journeyman exam

Passed journeyman exam with the award journeyman of the year.

Approved by the Swedish Agricultural Agency

More abot me

I have devoted a large part of my life to horses since I was eight years old. I started at riding school and then got my own horses and have also for many years trained foals into adult horses. I have worked in stables both in Sweden and Denmark with everything from stable maintenance and riding to assistant and stable manager.

In 2009, I started investing in farriery full-time. I first went through a two-year apprenticeship at the Academy of Crafts and had the privilege of being apprenticed to one of Sweden's best farriers. After that, I furthered my education at the Biological Vocational College in Skara. To then take my journeyman test and receive the journeyman of the year award. In order to continue to develop, I regularly attend courses and compete in farriery.

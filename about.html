About me

Where I work

My work area is Västergötland and Örebro county, starting from Töreboda. I shoe and process hooves on all types of horses, from hobby and forest horses to horses that compete at a high level in all disciplines.

Education

Academy of Crafts, Stockholm, 2 years

The vocational school Academy of Crafts has developed a modern craft education with clear guidelines and a solid apprenticeship.

Biological Vocational School, Skara, 1 year

BYS farrier school in Skara was the first in Sweden to be certified by EFFA, the European Federation of Farrier Association. They continue to be at the forefront of teaching and state-of-the-art technology, as they have been since the school was founded in the 18th century.

Journeyman exam

  • Passed journeyman exam with the award journeyman of the year.
  • Approved by the Swedish Agricultural Agency.

More about me

I have devoted a large part of my life to horses since I was eight years old. First I went to riding school, and later it was a natural urge to acquire my first horse. For some years, I trained foals into adult horses. I have worked in stables both in Sweden and Denmark with everything from stable maintenance and riding to assistant and stable manager.

In 2009, I started investing in farrier profession full-time. I first went through a two-year apprenticeship at the Academy of Crafts and had the privilege of being apprenticed to one of Sweden's best farriers. I then furthered my education at the University of Applied Sciences in Skara. To then take my journeyman exam and receive the journeyman of the year award. To continue developing, I regularly attend courses and competitions for farriers.

About me

Wher I work

My work area is Västergötland and Örebro County, starting from Töreboda. I shoe and work all types of horses from hobby/forest horses to high level competition horses in all disciplines

Education

Hantverksakademin Stockholm 2 years

Yrkeshögskolan Hantverksakademin är en modern lärlingsutbildning, där vi har tagit det bästa från århundraden av hantverksutbildning och anpassat till 2000-talet.

Biologiska Yrkeshögskolan Skara 1 year

BYS farrier school in Skara dates back to the 18th century, Sweden's oldest farrier school. First in Sweden to be certified by EFFA, the European Federation of Farriers Association.

Journeyman exam

Passed journeyman exam with the award journeyman of the year.

Approved by the Swedish Agricultural Agency

More abot me

A solid horse girl who has been involved with horses since I was eight years old. Both at riding school, with several own horses and worked in several different stables. Both in Sweden and Denmark. With everything from stable maintenance and riding to assistant and stable manager. I currently have two young horses that I am training.

Since the beginning of 2009, I have invested in farriery full-time. First, two years of apprenticeship at the Academy of Crafts with one of Sweden's best farriers. Then a year of further education at the Biological Vocational College in Skara. In June 2014, I also took a journeyman's certificate in a butcher shop. To keep myself as up-to-date as possible, I regularly compete in farriery and attend courses and clinics.
